Who We Are

Helping Hands is a community-driven organization dedicated to addressing food insecurity and supporting vulnerable individuals and families in Port Elizabeth (Gqeberha). Since our founding in 2012, we have been committed to not just providing immediate relief, but also empowering people with the tools and skills they need to build better futures.

We believe that hunger is not just about food - it's about dignity, community, and hope. Every Thursday, our volunteers come together to serve warm meals and offer a welcoming space where everyone is treated with respect and compassion.
