Our Story

Helping Hands was founded in 2012 by a small group of concerned community members who witnessed the growing need for food assistance in Port Elizabeth. What started as a weekend soup kitchen has grown into a comprehensive support network serving hundreds of families each month.

Our Vision

"A hunger-free Gqeberha where every person has access to nutritious food and the opportunity to thrive."

Our Mission

To feed and support the homeless and vulnerable in Port Elizabeth by providing regular meals, essential items, and skills development opportunities that promote dignity and self-sufficiency.
