Get Involved

There are many ways to support our mission of creating a hunger-free Gqeberha. Whether you can volunteer your time, sponsor a meal, or partner with us in other ways, every contribution makes a difference.

Volunteer Opportunities

Meal Preparation & Serving

Help prepare and serve meals every Thursday evening. Perfect for individuals or groups looking to make a direct impact.

Clothing Sorting & Distribution

Sort donated clothing and help with monthly distribution events. Great for volunteers who prefer daytime activities.

Skills Workshop Leadership

Share your professional expertise by leading workshops in job skills, computer literacy, or life skills.

Administrative Support

Help with social media, event planning, fundraising, and general organizational tasks.

Sponsorship Opportunities

Businesses and individuals can sponsor our programs in various ways:

  • Meal Sponsorship: R500 feeds 25 people for one Thursday
  • Monthly Program Sponsorship: R2000 covers all weekly meals for a month
  • Event Sponsorship: Support special events like back-to-school drives
  • Equipment Donations: Kitchen equipment, serving supplies, or transportation

Corporate Partnerships

We welcome partnerships with businesses that share our values. Corporate partners can:

  • Organize employee volunteer days
  • Sponsor specific programs or events
  • Provide in-kind donations of goods or services
  • Offer skills training in their area of expertise
  • Support our fundraising events
