Our Locations

Central Distribution Point

Address:
Community Hall
123 Church Street
Central, Port Elizabeth, 6001

Services:
Thursday meal distribution (5:00 PM - 7:00 PM)
Monthly clothing distribution (First Saturday, 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM)

Best for: City center residents, individuals, families in the central area

Transport: Near Church Street taxi rank, walking distance from city center

Township Community Center

Address:
Ubuntu Community Center
45 Mandela Avenue
New Brighton, Port Elizabeth, 6205

Services:
Thursday meal distribution (5:00 PM - 7:00 PM)
Skills workshops (Second Saturday, 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

Best for: Township residents, skills training participants

Transport: Accessible by local taxi routes, free parking available

Find Us

Both of our locations are easily accessible by public transport and have parking available. We welcome walk-ins during our service hours, but feel free to call ahead if you have specific questions.

Directions

To Central Distribution Point

From PE City Center: Walk down Church Street towards the harbor. The Community Hall is on your left, opposite the old Post Office building.

To Township Community Center

Take the New Brighton taxi from Greenacres or Central taxi rank. Ask the driver for Mandela Avenue - the Ubuntu Center is the large community building near the clinic.
